# Stakeholder Personas

This document describes the key stakeholders for the **Palthanio Home Insurance Analytics** solution.

---

# CFO

### Focus

Financial performance and profitability.

### Key Questions

- What is the combined ratio?
- Are we profitable?
- Are expenses increasing?

### KPIs Used

- Earned Premium
- Combined Ratio
- Expense Ratio
- Underwriting Profit

---

# Head of Underwriting

### Focus

Risk quality and pricing strategy.

### Questions

- Which risks have poor loss ratios?
- Are high-risk properties increasing?

### KPIs

- Loss Ratio
- High Risk Property Share
- Average Premium
- Policy Count

---

# Claims Manager

### Focus

Claims cost and severity.

### Questions

- Which claims cost the most?
- How large are outstanding reserves?

### KPIs

- Claims Paid
- Outstanding Reserves
- Incurred Claims
- Claim Count

---

# Broker Relationship Manager

### Focus

Broker performance and portfolio quality.

### Questions

- Which brokers bring profitable business?
- Which brokers generate losses?

### KPIs

- Broker Premium
- Broker Contribution %
- Loss Ratio

---

# Portfolio Analyst

### Focus

Trend analysis and reporting insights.

### Responsibilities

- identifying patterns
- preparing insight reports
- supporting executives
