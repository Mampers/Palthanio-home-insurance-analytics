# Business Requirements

## Project Purpose

The **Palthanio Home Insurance Analytics** project simulates how a home insurance company could analyse portfolio performance using modern BI tools.

The solution enables stakeholders to understand profitability, risk exposure, broker performance, and claims trends.

---

## Business Problem

Insurance companies need visibility into:

- underwriting profitability
- claims experience
- broker performance
- expense management
- portfolio risk exposure

Without structured analytics, these insights are difficult to produce consistently.

---

## Project Objectives

The project aims to:

- provide a unified analytics model
- support executive decision making
- analyse broker performance
- evaluate claims trends
- identify unprofitable portfolio segments
- demonstrate real-world BI modelling

---

## In Scope

The project includes:

- policy portfolio analysis
- broker performance
- premium analysis
- claims paid analysis
- claim reserves analysis
- expense analysis
- insurance KPI reporting
- Power BI dashboards

---

## Out of Scope

The following are excluded:

- real-time policy administration systems
- actuarial reserving models
- machine learning pricing models
- fraud detection systems
- regulatory solvency reporting

---

## Key Business Questions

### Executive Questions

- Is the portfolio profitable?
- What is the combined ratio trend?
- Which segments drive the most losses?

### Underwriting Questions

- Which property types have high loss ratios?
- Are high-risk policies increasing?

### Claims Questions

- Which claim types cost the most?
- Are claims rising in specific regions?

### Broker Questions

- Which brokers generate the most premium?
- Which brokers produce profitable business?

### Finance Questions

- What is the expense ratio?
- What is the underwriting profit?

---

## Reporting Requirements

The dashboard should provide:

- Executive Summary
- Portfolio Performance Trends
- Broker Analysis
- Claims Analysis
- Policy Segmentation
- Financial Ratio Analysis

---

## Data Requirements

The solution requires data covering:

- policies
- brokers
- addresses
- premium transactions
- claim payments
- claim reserves
- operating expenses
- risk attributes

---

## Success Criteria

The project succeeds if it:

- delivers a clean dimensional model
- supports accurate KPI reporting
- demonstrates strong BI engineering
- supports interview discussion
