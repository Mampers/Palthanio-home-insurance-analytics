# Power BI Measures Documentation
## Palthanio Home Insurance Analytics

This document explains the key **DAX measures used in the Power BI semantic model** for the Palthanio Home Insurance Analytics project.

These measures power the **Executive Summary, Claims Analysis, Portfolio Analysis, and Underwriting dashboards**, enabling stakeholders to monitor insurance portfolio performance.

---

# Executive Dashboard Measures

## Total Net Premium
**Definition**

Represents the total premium retained by the insurer after policy adjustments.

**Purpose**

Provides a view of the **actual underwriting revenue** generated by the portfolio.

**Used In**

- Executive Summary KPI
- Broker Performance Analysis
- Portfolio Performance

---

## Total Claims Paid
**Definition**

Total amount paid out for claims.

**Purpose**

Shows the **cost of insurance losses** during the reporting period.

**Used In**

- Executive Summary KPI
- Claims Analysis
- Claim Type Analysis

---

## No of Active Policies
**Definition**

Counts all currently active policies within the portfolio.

**Purpose**

Indicates the **size of the insurer's policy portfolio**.

**Used In**

- Executive Summary KPI
- Portfolio Analysis

---

## No of Claims
**Definition**

Counts all claims recorded in the system.

**Purpose**

Measures **claims activity and portfolio risk exposure**.
