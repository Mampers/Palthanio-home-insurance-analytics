# Power BI Measures Documentation
## Palthanio Home Insurance Analytics

This document explains the key **DAX measures used in the Power BI semantic model** for the Palthanio Home Insurance Analytics project.

These measures power the **Executive Summary, Claims Analysis, Portfolio Analysis, and Underwriting dashboards**, enabling stakeholders to monitor insurance portfolio performance.

---

# Core Insurance KPIs

## Loss Ratio

**Definition**

Measures claims cost relative to premium revenue.

**Formula**

```text
Loss Ratio = Total Claims Paid / Total Net Premium
```

**Purpose**

One of the most important insurance metrics. It indicates whether the insurer is paying out more in claims than it earns in premium.

**Interpretation**

| Value | Meaning |
|------|------|
| < 60% | Highly profitable |
| 60–80% | Healthy insurance portfolio |
| 80–100% | Reduced profitability |
| > 100% | Loss-making portfolio |

---

## Claims Frequency

**Definition**

Measures how often claims occur relative to policy count.

**Formula**

```text
Claims Frequency = Number of Claims / Number of Policies
```

**Purpose**

Helps identify **risk exposure in the portfolio**.

Higher values may indicate:

- Riskier properties
- Underwriting issues
- High-risk regions

---

## Average Claim Cost

**Definition**

Average value of each claim.

**Formula**

```text
Average Claim Cost = Total Claims Paid / Number of Claims
```

**Purpose**

Measures **claims severity**.

Helps identify:

- High cost claim types
- Expensive regions
- Structural risk exposure

---

## Combined Expenses

**Definition**

Total operational costs associated with managing the insurance portfolio.

**Includes**

- Administrative costs
- Operational expenses
- Distribution costs

**Purpose**

Used alongside the **Loss Ratio** to calculate the **Combined Ratio**.

---

# Loss & Profitability Metrics

## Expense Ratio

**Definition**

Measures operational costs relative to earned premium.

**Formula**

```text
Expense Ratio = Operating Expenses / Earned Premium
```

**Purpose**

Evaluates **operational efficiency**.

---

## Combined Ratio

**Definition**

Overall profitability indicator for insurers.

**Formula**

```text
Combined Ratio = Loss Ratio + Expense Ratio
```

**Purpose**

Measures total underwriting profitability.

| Value | Interpretation |
|------|------|
| < 100% | Profitable |
| = 100% | Break-even |
| > 100% | Loss-making |

---

## Loss Ratio Colour Band

**Definition**

Categorises loss ratio into performance bands.

**Example**

| Band | Interpretation |
|----|----|
| Green | Healthy |
| Amber | Moderate risk |
| Red | Unprofitable |

**Purpose**

Used for **conditional formatting in dashboards**.

# Portfolio Risk Metrics

## High & Very High Risk Bands Count

**Definition**

Counts policies classified as **High Risk** or **Very High Risk**.

**Purpose**

Helps underwriters identify concentrations of high-risk policies.

---

## High Risk Property Share

**Definition**

Percentage of policies that fall within high-risk categories.

**Purpose**

Used to evaluate **portfolio risk exposure**.

---

## High Risk / Very High Risk Property Share

**Definition**

Percentage of policies classified as **High Risk or Very High Risk** relative to the total portfolio.

**Purpose**

Helps insurers monitor **risk concentration** within the portfolio.

---

# Claims Severity Metrics

## Largest Claim Cost

**Definition**

Highest individual claim value recorded.

**Purpose**

Helps identify **extreme loss events** or catastrophic claims.

---

## Median Claim Cost

**Definition**

The middle claim value when all claims are ordered by value.

**Purpose**

Provides a **more stable measure of claim severity** than averages when extreme claims exist.

---

## Severe Claim Share %

**Definition**

Percentage of claims classified as severe or high-cost.

**Purpose**

Helps identify potential catastrophic exposure.

---

## Severe Claim Cost Share %

**Definition**

Proportion of total claims cost generated by severe claims.

**Purpose**

Shows whether a small number of claims drive the majority of losses.

---

# Broker Performance Metrics

## Broker Premium

**Definition**

Total premium generated by each broker.

**Purpose**

Used to evaluate:

- Distribution channel performance
- Broker contribution to revenue

---

## Broker Claims Paid

**Definition**

Claims cost attributed to policies sold by each broker.

**Purpose**

Helps assess the **risk quality of broker portfolios**.

---

## Broker Loss Ratio

**Definition**

Measures profitability of broker portfolios.

**Formula**

```text
Broker Loss Ratio = Broker Claims Paid / Broker Premium
```

**Purpose**

Evaluates whether brokers are delivering profitable business.

---

## Average Policies per Broker

**Definition**

Average number of policies sold per broker.

**Purpose**

Measures **broker productivity and engagement**.

---

# Portfolio Metrics

## No of Policies

**Definition**

Total number of policies in the portfolio.

---

## No of Unique Policies

**Definition**

Distinct policy count used to avoid duplicates when multiple transactions exist.

---

## No of Unique Brokers

**Definition**

Total number of brokers distributing policies.

---

# Property Exposure Metrics

## Average Rebuild Cost

**Definition**

Average rebuild value of insured properties.

**Purpose**

Provides insight into **property exposure and insured risk value**.

---

## Total Rebuild Cost

**Definition**

Total rebuild value across the insured portfolio.

**Purpose**

Helps insurers understand **total financial exposure**.

---

## Average Premium

**Definition**

Average premium charged per policy.

---

## Average Premium per Rebuild Cost

**Definition**

Measures pricing relative to property value.

**Formula**

```text
Average Premium per Rebuild Cost = Average Premium / Average Rebuild Cost
```

**Purpose**

Evaluates whether the insurer is **pricing policies appropriately relative to property risk**.

---

# System Measures

## Last Refresh Date

**Definition**

Displays the last dataset refresh timestamp.

**Purpose**

Ensures transparency and confirms **data freshness for report consumers**.

---

# Summary

The measures in this semantic model support four key analytical themes:

1. **Insurance Financial Performance**
2. **Claims Behaviour and Severity**
3. **Broker Distribution Performance**
4. **Portfolio Risk Monitoring**

Together, these metrics allow insurers to monitor profitability, evaluate underwriting quality, and identify portfolio risks.
